Comments
========

 - I can add comments with markers
 - Comments are id'ed by article and an integer, i.e. article/1, article/2
 - Within markdown I simply mark comments by their ID (<!-- 1 -->)
 - When in Draft mode, comments are displayed on the side
 - Comment Entity: Content, Author, Status (New, Accepted, Rejected)
 - Addition: Comments can be threaded; Questions asked
 - Email-Notification when a comment is added...
